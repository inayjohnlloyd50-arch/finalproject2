Our Menu

Signature Flavors

Choose from our 8 mouth-watering sauces that we have in our menu

Honey Glazed

A sweet and savory glaze combining honey with soy sauce for umami, garlic for flavor, and a touch of heat from Sriracha or hot sauce.

Korean Teriyaki

Inspired by Japanese teriyaki, this features a sweet soy sauce base with Korean twists like garlic, ginger, and optional spiciness from gochujang.

Butter Garlic

Rich and savory, centered on melted butter and fresh garlic for a classic, comforting flavor that's perfect for any occasion.

Sour Cream & Onion

Creamy and tangy, mimicking the beloved chip flavor with sour cream, onion powder, and herbs for a cool, crunchy contrast.

Spicy Buffalo

The classic hot sauce with a tangy kick, made from cayenne pepper sauce, vinegar, and butter for that perfect heat and flavor balance.

Cheesy Cheese

Indulgent and gooey, featuring a blend of cheddar and cream cheese for a rich, melt-in-your-mouth experience.

BBQ Classic

Smoky and sweet, made with tomato paste, vinegar, molasses, and spices for that traditional barbecue taste.

Crispy Ranch

Herbaceous and creamy, with buttermilk, dill, and garlic for a cool, zesty dip that's great with crispy wings.
